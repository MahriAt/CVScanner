Job Description

In this role, you will:

Be responsible for implementing and maintaining the user interface components of our applications.
Ensure a seamless and visually appealing user experience.
Focus on using React to build robust and scalable frontend solutions.

Qualifications

About The Role:

We are seeking a Frontend Developer to join our Development team. This role might be the perfect fit for you if;

Minimum of 2 years of professional experience with React.js.
You have a Bachelor's or higher degree from the CS Engineering department
Strong proficiency in JavaScript/Typescript, HTML, and CSS.
Extensive experience with React and its core principles.
Knowledge of frontend build tools and package managers, such as Webpack and npm.
Familiarity with version control systems, particularly Git.
Solid understanding of responsive design and mobile-first development principles.
Experience with RESTful APIs and integrating frontend components with backend services.
Nice to have: Experience with Wordpress and familiarity with its theming system.
Nice to have: Knowledge of CSS preprocessors, such as Sass or LESS.
